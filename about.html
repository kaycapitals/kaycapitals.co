About

Somesh

Full-time day trader. Founder of Kay Capitals University. No finance degree, no Wall Street connections, no trust fund. Just screen time, discipline, and an obsession with the markets.

I moved to Australia at 18 as a first-generation immigrant with nothing. Today I trade US stock market options from the Gold Coast and run one of the fastest-growing trading mentorship programs in the world.

The Trading Journey

I started trading over seven years ago with money I genuinely couldn't afford to lose. I blew accounts. I revenge traded. I made every mistake you've probably made too. The difference is I didn't quit.

I kept showing up, kept studying, kept journaling every single trade. Eventually the losses turned into lessons and the lessons turned into consistency. I went full-time and never looked back.

Today I trade US stock market options every single market day from my studio on the Gold Coast, Australia. SPY, QQQ, NVDA, TSLA, AAPL. I trade live in front of my students. I show the wins. I show the losses. No hiding.

Trading Philosophy

I don't believe in get-rich-quick. I believe in get-rich-disciplined. Here's what I stand on:

  • Risk management above everything. If you can't manage risk, nothing else matters.
  • Process over P&L. Focus on executing your system, not checking your balance every 5 minutes.
  • Patience is a competitive advantage. The best traders wait. The worst traders chase.
  • Radical honesty. I show red days. I show blown trades. If you can't be real about where you are, you can't get where you want to go.
  • Psychology is 80% of trading. Strategy is the easy part. Controlling your mind is the real game.

Why I Started Kay Capitals University

When I hit 1M followers on Instagram, I realised something uncomfortable: most trading "education" online is complete garbage. Rented Lamborghinis, fake P&L screenshots, and $97 courses that teach you nothing.

I wanted to build the mentorship program I wish I had when I started. Something real. Something that actually teaches you how to trade, how to manage risk, and how to handle the psychology that destroys 90% of traders.

KCU isn't a course. It's a mentorship. Live trading sessions. Direct feedback. A community of traders who hold each other accountable. No hype. No shortcuts. Just the work.
