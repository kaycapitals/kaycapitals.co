If you're reading this, you're probably doing your homework. Good. You should be. Dropping money on any education program without knowing exactly what you're getting into is how people get burned. What Is Kay Capitals University?

KCU is a 6-month day trading options mentorship program. Not a course. Not a Discord with alerts you blindly follow. A mentorship.

That distinction matters. A course gives you information. A mentorship gives you transformation. There's a massive difference between knowing what a call option is and actually being able to trade SPY 0DTE options with discipline, consistency, and a real edge.

The program runs for six months because that's roughly how long it takes to rewire the habits that are keeping you stuck. You didn't develop bad trading habits overnight — you're not going to fix them in a weekend webinar.

Here's what the 6-month structure looks like:

  • Month 1-2: Foundation — understanding options mechanics, how I read price action on SPY, QQQ, NVDA, TSLA, building your trading plan, risk management rules from day one
  • Month 3-4: Execution — live trading alongside me, real-time analysis, learning to pull the trigger without hesitation and cut losses without ego
  • Month 5-6: Independence — you're trading on your own with oversight, refining your system, building the consistency that turns this into a career

The Curriculum: What You Actually Learn

I'm not going to sugarcoat this — the curriculum is intense. It's designed to be. Trading isn't supposed to feel comfortable when you're learning. If it does, you're not being challenged enough.

  • Technical Foundation:
  • Options pricing and Greeks (not textbook theory — practical application)
  • Reading price action and market structure on SPY and QQQ
  • Identifying high-probability setups I use daily
  • Understanding 0DTE options and how to trade them without gambling
  • Volume analysis, support/resistance, and momentum shifts

Risk Management Framework: This is where most programs fall short and where KCU is different. I don't just tell you to "manage your risk." I give you the exact rules. Position sizing formulas. Maximum daily loss limits. When to stop trading for the day. When to size up. When to walk away. This alone is worth the entire program — I talk about this in depth in my trading risk management strategies post.

Psychology and Mindset: Probably 60% of your success in trading comes down to what's happening between your ears. We cover revenge trading, FOMO, fear of pulling the trigger, overtrading, and the emotional spirals that drain accounts. I've built specific frameworks for handling each of these — not motivational fluff, but actual protocols you follow when your emotions are screaming at you. More on this in my piece on day trading psychology tips.

The Trading System: You get access to the Kay Capitals trading system — the actual methodology I use to trade every single day. The setups, the entries, the exits, the rules. It's not some black-box indicator. It's a repeatable, rules-based approach that you learn to execute with precision.

Trading Plan Templates: The exact frameworks I use for my own trading plan, adapted for students at different account sizes.
